Awyr Dant - mountain of legend. Site of centuries of war between the archetypal enemies Elves and Dwarves. Surrounded by hundreds of square miles of forest the elves hold so dear, yet, at its core, solid rock that no sane dwarf would ever give up. Long have armies fought on its slopes, blood running in thick rivulets down its broad slopes. Heroes were born and legends died fighting for their cause in vast groves and caverns.


On Awyn Dant's northern slope, the great dwarven city of Corachod was finally captured by the elven baron Calci close to 50 years ago. Calci razed Corachad the ground, disgusted with the unpleasing harsh lines and stone. In spite, he used dwarven slaves to rebuild the city in a style more suitable to elves, naming it Samot'cis.
